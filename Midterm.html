The Trees In Camden, London

This map represents of the trees in the London Borough of Camden. I was inspired by Jill Hubley's NYC Tree map.

I was curious to see what the tree canopy would look like in London, but due to availabity of data I was only able to show the tree canopy of the Borough of Camden.

The colors represent the type of tree and if you hover over them, you will be able to see the common name of the tree.

There are 310 different types of trees in Camden.
